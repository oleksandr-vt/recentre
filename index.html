Get back to full strength postpartum by strengthening your pelvic floor and reversing symptoms like diastasis, pelvic organ prolapse and incontinence.

re-centre is a platform focusing on Hypopressives that integrates Yoga and Pilates to deliver a complete postpartum programme.

Hypopressives is a low impact breathing and whole body exercise technique that builds strength in your pelvic floor and helps overcome postnatal issues.

What is hypopressives and how does it work?

Hypopressives is a low impact breathing and exercise technique that works to reduce pressure in the pelvic and abdominal cavities. This lifts the pelvic organs and stimulates a contraction of the pelvic floor and deep core muscles, strongly increasing their muscular activity.

Hypopressives improves:

  • Pelvic organ prolapse
  • Incontinence
  • Diastasis recti
  • Poor posture
  • Pelvic floor weakness
  • Metabolism
  • Sexual function
  • Waist circumference
  • Respiratory issues
  • Back pain
  • Digestion
